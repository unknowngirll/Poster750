# Metagenomic Analysis of Microbiomes: A Bioinformatics Workflow

The companion documentation for our LIFE750 Team Scientific Poster. This repository provides a transparent, fully reproducible log of the computational methods, scripts, and analytical outputs generated during our study.

## Project Overview
In alignment with the core objectives of the module assessment, this project demonstrates how raw metagenomic sequencing data is computationally processed and analyzed to generate biologically meaningful insights. The emphasis of this documentation is on the rigorous design of the bioinformatics workflow and the analytical justification for each tool selected.

## Workflow Structure
To ensure reproducibility and logical progression, our analysis follows the structured methodology presented in our poster:

* **Background & Dataset:** Overview of the study design and raw sequence acquisition.
* **Quality Control:** Stringent pre-processing and read filtering to ensure high-fidelity downstream analysis.
* **Taxonomic Profiling:** K-mer based microbial classification (Kraken2/Bracken) to determine community structure.
* **Statistical Biomarkers:** Differential abundance analysis to identify significant community shifts.
* **Assembly & Annotation:** *De novo* assembly of complex metagenomes to reconstruct functional elements.
